Effects of ProsperLoan Characteristics on Its APR (Annual Percentage Rate)¶

by: Abubakar Keita¶

Investigation Overview¶

The main purpose of this investigation is to summarize the characteristics of variables that could affect the loan APR. In this investigation, I looked at the attributes of the Prosper loan that could predict the annual percentage rate (ARP). Four main features that I thought could help me in my investigation are prosperRating, amount, debt-to-income ratio, listingCategory.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set contains approximately 113,937 loan listings with 81 loan features. The features include loan amount, borrower's rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, prosperRating, debt-to-income ratio, listingCategory and others.

Distribution of Borrower's APR¶

  • The borrower's APR in the prosper loan dataset has continuous values that range from 0.006530 to 0.512290 with 25%, 50%, and 75% of the data concetrated at the rate of 0.156290, 0.209760 and 0.283810 respectfively. Plotted with a standard scale, the distribution of borrower;s APR takes on a multimodal shape with a peak at 0.36.
